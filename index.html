About Us

Trusted Bumiputera engineering consultancy since 2000.

Perunding Ihsan Sdn Bhd is a multidisciplinary firm specialising in civil & structural, mechanical & electrical, project management and advisory services. We support projects from feasibility study & design through to construction supervision and completion.

Our team has delivered highways, bridges, housing developments, industrial facilities, water supply schemes and coastal protection works across Malaysia, working closely with public and private sector clients.

Core values

Integrity, technical excellence, safety and sustainable design in every project.

Leadership

Led by experienced professional engineers registered with the Board of Engineers Malaysia.
