Highways & Transportation

Upgrading of Federal Road & Interchange Works

Client Government agency Scope Detailed design & supervision Location Peninsular Malaysia

Dual carriageway upgrading with new grade-separated interchange, realignment of existing junctions and associated drainage and slope protection works.

  • Geometric design for mainline, ramps and local access roads.
  • Pavement design, road safety furniture and traffic management planning.
  • Construction supervision and coordination with local authorities.
Bridge & Structural Works
02 / 08

River Bridge & Approach Road Improvement

Client State public works department Scope Detailed design Location Malaysia

Design of a new multi-span reinforced concrete bridge and realigned approaches to replace an existing substandard crossing.

  • Structural analysis of bridge superstructure and substructure.
  • Hydraulic assessment of river flow and scour protection details.
  • Design of approach embankments, retaining walls and drainage.
Urban Roads & Access
03 / 08

Access Road to Industrial Development

Client Private developer Scope Study & detailed design Location Malaysia

New access connection from a federal route into an industrial estate, including junction upgrades and internal circulation.

  • Traffic impact study and capacity analysis for existing junction.
  • Design of turning lanes, roundabout / signalised junction options.
  • Drainage, street lighting and utility coordination.
Township & Infrastructure

Infrastructure for Mixed Development Township

Client Property developer Scope Full infrastructure design Location Malaysia

Platform earthworks, drainage, water supply, sewerage and road network design for a multi-phase residential and commercial township.

  • Earthworks and platform level optimisation.
  • Stormwater drainage network and detention / retention facilities.
  • Utilities corridors and road hierarchy planning.

Civil & Structural Works for Industrial Plant

Client Industrial operator Scope Structural & civil design Location Malaysia

Structural and civil engineering design for an industrial processing facility, including building frames, equipment foundations and external works.

River Improvement & Flood Relief Channel

Client Local authority Scope Detailed design & supervision Location Malaysia

Channel improvement and construction of a relief channel to reduce flooding risk in a developed catchment.

Coastal Revetment & Access Road

Client Government agency Scope Design & supervision Location Coastal Malaysia

Design and supervision of rock revetment works and associated access road to protect eroding shoreline.
