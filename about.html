Company overview

Grounded in engineering rigour, focused on real-world project delivery.

Perunding Ihsan provides end-to-end consulting services covering feasibility studies, preliminary and detailed design, tender documentation, contract administration and site supervision.

The practice has been involved in highways, interchanges, bridges, buildings, industrial plants, townships, drainage systems, water supply and coastal protection works across Malaysia.

What clients can expect

• Clear, practical recommendations backed by analysis
• Designs that consider safety, constructability and lifecycle cost
• Close coordination with authorities, contractors and stakeholders
• Active project management from concept through completion

How we work

Engineering practice built on quality, safety and collaboration.

The firm combines strong technical capabilities with hands-on project management to keep programmes, budgets and design quality on track.

Technical excellence

Designs are prepared in accordance with relevant Malaysian and international standards, supported by in-house analysis tools for highways, structures, drainage and infrastructure.

Safety & constructability

Solutions take into account site conditions, construction staging, traffic management and long-term operations to ensure projects can be built and maintained safely.

Client-focused delivery

Project teams maintain clear communication with clients, consultants and contractors, providing timely updates and documentation for every project stage.
