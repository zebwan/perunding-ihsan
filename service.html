Consulting & project services

End-to-end engineering services from feasibility studies to construction supervision.

Perunding Ihsan provides a full range of civil, structural, highway, infrastructure and marine engineering services to support public and private sector projects throughout Malaysia.

Whether the project is a new expressway, bridge, industrial facility or coastal protection work, our team can assist from early planning through detailed design, tender, contract administration and site supervision.

Core consulting services

Services designed to support every stage of an infrastructure project.

The scope of work can be tailored to a full design-and-supervise appointment or individual tasks such as feasibility study, detail design, independent checking or construction supervision.

01 · Project management
Project management & coordination
  • Overall planning, scheduling and progress monitoring.
  • Coordination between client, authorities, consultants and contractor.
  • Regular technical and progress reporting.
02 · Studies
Feasibility & engineering studies
  • Technical and site assessment for proposed routes and locations.
  • Traffic, capacity and safety studies for roads and interchanges.
  • Preliminary cost estimates and implementation strategies.
03 · Design
Preliminary & detailed design
  • Geometric design of highways, interchanges and access roads.
  • Structural design of bridges, retaining walls and buildings.
  • Earthworks, drainage, water supply and utilities design.
04 · Documentation
Tender documentation & contract admin
  • Preparation of drawings, BOQ, specifications and tender documents.
  • Assistance during tender calling, evaluation and recommendation.
  • Contract administration, variation evaluation and technical advice.
05 · Site services
Construction supervision & site inspection
  • Resident engineer / clerk of works on site as required.
  • Monitoring of workmanship, materials and compliance with drawings.
  • Site meetings, inspections and completion / handover support.
06 · Building audit
Building condition & structural assessment
  • Visual inspection and structural review of existing buildings.
  • Identification of defects and proposed remedial solutions.
  • Technical reports for owners, insurers or authorities.
Sector-specific solutions

Specialised services across highways, structures, infrastructure and marine works.

In addition to general civil and structural design, Perunding Ihsan offers focused expertise in the following sectors.

Sector Typical projects Key services
Highways & transportation Expressways, interchanges, urban roads, access roads, traffic improvement schemes. Route selection, traffic analysis, geometric & pavement design, drainage, safety audits, staging and supervision.
Structural & building works Bridges, flyovers, retaining structures, commercial & industrial buildings. Structural analysis, foundation and superstructure design, detailing review, strengthening recommendations and site support.
Infrastructure & utilities Platform earthworks, drainage networks, water supply, sewerage and utility corridors. Earthworks and platform design, stormwater management, water and sewer networks, coordination of utility diversions and protection.
Marine & coastal engineering Revetments, seawalls, jetties, coastal access roads and protection works. Concept and detailed design, coastal protection structures, associated road and drainage systems, plus supervision during construction.
