Technical expertise

Engineering expertise built on real project delivery.

Perunding Ihsan combines civil, structural, highway, infrastructure and marine engineering capabilities with hands-on project management, contract administration and supervision.

The firm’s experience ranges from feasibility studies and planning through detailed design, tender documentation and construction support, allowing projects to be delivered safely, efficiently and in line with Malaysian standards.

Practice disciplines

Disciplines that support transport, building, infrastructure and coastal projects.

Perunding Ihsan’s technical scope covers project management and the full range of civil and structural design work needed to deliver public and private sector projects across Malaysia.

Project management & supervision
Project leadership and coordination

Coordination of multi-disciplinary design teams, programming of activities, reporting to clients and authorities, and monitoring of progress, quality and risk from commencement to completion.

Highways & transportation
Roads, interchanges and access

Planning and design of expressways, interchanges, urban roads and access routes including traffic analysis, geometric design, pavement design and roadside safety features.

Structural & buildings
Bridges, buildings and retaining structures

Structural design of bridges, flyovers, buildings and retaining structures, covering foundations, superstructure, stability checks and constructability for permanent and temporary works.

Infrastructure & utilities
Earthworks, drainage and services

Platform earthworks, road platforms, stormwater drainage, culverts and utility corridors to support townships, industrial facilities and mixed developments.

Marine & coastal
Revetments and coastal protection

Concept and detailed design of revetments, seawalls, jetties, access roads and associated drainage systems for coastal and waterfront developments.

Building assessment
Condition surveys and strengthening

Visual assessments, structural evaluation and recommendations for remedial and strengthening works to support building owners and operators.

Service capabilities

Capabilities that cover the full life cycle of an engineering project.

The firm provides consulting services from early feasibility through to construction supervision so that clients can engage a single team for technical, contractual and on-site support.

Feasibility studies & planning
  • Pre-investment studies and option assessments for proposed routes and sites.
  • Technical, environmental and land use considerations for highways and infrastructure.
  • High-level cost estimates and implementation strategies to support decision making.
Engineering design
  • Topographic survey and subsoil investigation inputs for design.
  • Detailed design of alignments, structures, drainage and platforms.
  • Preparation of design drawings, technical specifications and cost estimates.
Tender arrangements & contract administration
  • Preparation of tender documents and schedules of quantities.
  • Tender evaluation and recommendations to clients.
  • Contract administration, variation assessments and technical advice during implementation.
Construction supervision & site services
  • Resident engineer / supervision team for highway, bridge and infrastructure works.
  • Monitoring of workmanship, materials and compliance with drawings and specifications.
  • Site meetings, inspections, testing and support during completion and handover.
Tools & standards

Design tools and standards used in delivering projects.

Analysis & design tools Use of specialised civil and structural engineering software alongside in-house tools for alignment geometry, earthworks quantities and structural analysis.
Documentation Digital production of drawings, schedules and tender documentation to support efficient review, coordination with authorities and clear communication with contractors.
Quality & coordination Internal checking procedures and coordination with architects, planners, M&E engineers and other specialists to maintain consistency across project stages.
